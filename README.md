## Purpose
This organization provides a central space for:

- developing and distributing research software and teaching materials  
- maintaining and publishing open-source projects of the university  
- fostering collaborative projects within the university  
- establishing common standards and best practices for software projects  

---

## Members
Participants may include:

- students, researchers, and staff of the university  
- external collaborators only with prior approval  
- access is managed by the University Library or central IT services
